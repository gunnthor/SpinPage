    ♦ About SpinPage ♦
    SpinPage

    A dramatic spinning wheel and random name picker for draft orders, giveaways, party games, and any moment that deserves a little spectacle. SpinPage turns a simple random picker into a polished casino-style experience with confetti, fanfare, plot twists, and just enough chaos to keep the table honest.

    Draft Orders
    Winners leave the wheel and line up in a clean pick list.
    Plot Twists
    Fake stops, illegal spins, and sudden boosts keep every pick theatrical.
    Zero Setup
    No accounts, no build step, no dependencies. Just add names and spin.
    Saved Locally
    Your current table survives refreshes using browser localStorage.
    What It Does
    • Spins a smooth animated canvas wheel to pick a random winner.
    • Builds a full draft order with undo, restore, clear, copy, and autospin controls.
    • Drops random power-up slices — Hot Seat, Cursed, and Wildcard — that swell or shrink the real odds.
    • Hides a ⚠ PANIC wedge that launches a horse race where only half the drafted survive.
    • Adds confetti, fire, ticks, fanfare, and a second-chance wheel where no one is safe.
    Best Used For
    • Fantasy drafts, giveaways, classroom picks, game nights, party orders, and dramatic tie-breakers.
    • Any decision that could technically be a spreadsheet, but deserves a spotlight instead.
